About Helios Inc.

Helios Inc. (legally Helios Robotics) builds precision robotic systems for industrial automation. The company is headquartered at Austin HQ, with a secondary engineering office in Berlin.

Leadership

  • Mara Vance — Founder and CEO
  • Priya Nair — Chief Financial Officer
  • Dana Okafor — VP Engineering
  • Sam Ortiz — Head of Sales
  • Leo Zhang — Lead Engineer, Project Atlas

Products

Helios ships three projects: Project Atlas (flagship robotic arm), Project Beacon (warehouse navigation), and Project Orion (research).

Investors

Helios Robotics is backed by Northwind Capital, which led the company's Series B.
