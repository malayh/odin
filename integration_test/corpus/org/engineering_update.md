# Engineering Update — Helios Robotics

From: Leo Zhang

## Project Atlas

The Atlas arm passed its precision benchmark this sprint. The remaining risk is the gripper firmware. Dana Okafor and I are aligned that Atlas is the team's top priority. I am still reconciling the Q2 commitment from the board with the Q3 date on the roadmap.

## Project Beacon

Beacon's navigation stack is stable in the test warehouse. It is ready for wider beta once Atlas frees up the perception team.

## Project Orion

Orion remains research-only. If we acquire Quanta Labs, their perception work would fold into Beacon first, then Orion.

## Team

The Berlin office now contributes to the Helios control platform. We are onboarding two engineers there next month.
