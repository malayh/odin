# Competitive Intel — Vertex Dynamics

From: Sam Ortiz, with input from the Atlas team.

## Positioning

Vertex Dynamics remains the competitor we encounter most in industrial-robotics deals. They undercut Helios on price but trail us on precision — the dimension Project Atlas is built to win.

## Funding & moves

Vertex Dynamics recently raised a large round and is hiring aggressively across Europe, which is part of why we are anchoring the Berlin office.

## Supply chain

Industry chatter is that Vertex Dynamics sources its actuators from Meridian Robotics — the same vendor behind the Atlas gripper. If that holds, both companies depend on a single supplier, and any Meridian disruption would hit Helios and Vertex alike.

## Open thread

The strategy offsite floated Vertex Dynamics as a possible partner on industry safety standards. That remains unresolved: a competitor in the field, a possible collaborator on standards.
