# Helios Robotics — 2026 Product Roadmap

Owner: Dana Okafor. Last updated by Leo Zhang.

## Project Atlas

Atlas is our flagship robotic arm for precision assembly. After the integration delays in the Berlin office, Project Atlas is now scheduled to ship in Q3. Leo Zhang leads Atlas engineering. Acme Logistics is the launch customer.

## Project Beacon

Beacon is our autonomous warehouse navigation stack. We are targeting general availability after Atlas. Beacon shares the perception pipeline we hope to strengthen via the Quanta Labs acquisition.

## Project Orion

Orion is an early-stage research effort into bipedal locomotion. Orion is exploratory and not yet committed to a ship date; Dana Okafor has flagged it as the most likely project to be cut if we need to focus.

## Platform

All three projects — Atlas, Beacon, and Orion — run on the shared Helios control platform maintained by Leo Zhang's team out of Austin HQ, with a growing contribution from the Berlin office.
