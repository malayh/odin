# Atlas Supply Chain Review — Helios Robotics

Prepared for the Atlas launch-readiness review. Owner: Nadia Rahman.

## Critical components

Project Atlas's gripper depends on high-precision actuators supplied by Meridian Robotics. Meridian Robotics is currently our **sole source** for these actuators; there is no qualified second supplier today.

The gripper firmware that Nadia Rahman's team owns is the highest remaining technical risk for the Atlas launch, and it is tightly coupled to the current Meridian actuator revision.

## Risk

A delay or shortage from Meridian Robotics would directly slip the Atlas ship date. Leo Zhang has asked us to qualify a backup actuator supplier before launch, but that work is not yet funded.
