# Helios Robotics — Q1 2026 Board Meeting

Date: March 12, 2026. Location: Austin HQ.

Attendees: Mara Vance (CEO), Priya Nair (CFO), Dana Okafor (CTO), and our lead partner from Northwind Capital.

## Financing

We closed our $40M Series B from Northwind Capital. Northwind Capital led the round and now holds a board seat. Priya Nair walked the board through the updated runway: at the current burn, the Series B extends our runway to roughly 30 months.

## Product

Dana Okafor, our CTO, reported that Project Atlas — our flagship robotic arm — is on track and will ship in Q2. Leo Zhang is leading the Atlas engineering team. Project Beacon, our warehouse navigation system, remains in beta.

## Go-to-market

Sam Ortiz, our Head of Sales, confirmed that Acme Logistics has signed as our first major customer for Project Atlas. Acme Logistics will pilot Atlas across two distribution centers.

## Competition

Vertex Dynamics remains our main competitor in industrial robotics. The board discussed Vertex Dynamics' recent funding and agreed Helios Robotics must accelerate the Atlas launch to stay ahead.

## Strategic

Mara Vance raised the possibility of acquiring Quanta Labs to bring their perception team in-house. Priya Nair will lead diligence.
