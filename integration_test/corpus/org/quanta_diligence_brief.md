# Quanta Labs — Diligence Brief (board-shared)

Prepared by Priya Nair for the Helios board. Scope: acquisition evaluation.

## Target

Quanta Labs was founded by Elena Sokolova, who built the company's perception stack. The diligence team's read is that two senior perception engineers — and Sokolova herself — are the real asset.

## Strategic fit

Quanta Labs' perception technology would fold into Project Beacon first, then later into Project Orion. It does not directly touch Project Atlas, though it competes for engineering attention during the Atlas launch.

## Deal & support

Priya Nair favors an all-stock structure. Leo Zhang is running the technical evaluation. Greta Holm — Northwind Capital's partner on the Helios board — has signaled that Northwind supports the acquisition.

## Open risks

Dana Okafor has flagged the distraction risk during the Atlas launch. Funding the deal may require delaying the Berlin office expansion by a quarter.
