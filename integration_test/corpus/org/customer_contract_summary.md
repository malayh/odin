# Acme Logistics — Contract Summary

Account owner: Sam Ortiz. Customer signatory: Rosa Méndez, VP Operations at Acme Logistics.

## Terms

- Acme Logistics is the launch customer for Project Atlas.
- Initial deployment: a two-site pilot across two Acme distribution centers.
- Expansion clause: Acme expands Atlas fleet-wide **only if** Atlas ships and clears its reliability targets by the **end of Q3 2026**.
- If Atlas misses the Q3 window, the expansion clause lapses and Acme may re-open the evaluation.

## Open dependency

Rosa Méndez has asked Sam Ortiz for a firm Atlas ship date. Engineering and the roadmap still disagree on Q2 versus Q3, so Sam has not been able to commit one. The expansion — the bulk of the contract's value — hinges on resolving that date.
