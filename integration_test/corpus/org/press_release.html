Helios Robotics Closes $45M Series B Led by Northwind Capital

AUSTIN — Helios Robotics, the precision-robotics company, today announced it has closed a $45M Series B financing round led by Northwind Capital. The round will fund the launch of Project Atlas, the company's flagship robotic arm.

"Northwind shares our vision for industrial autonomy," said Mara Vance, CEO of Helios Robotics. "This capital lets us bring Project Atlas to customers like Acme Logistics."

Helios will use the proceeds to expand its Berlin office and accelerate Project Beacon. A Northwind Capital partner joins the Helios board.
